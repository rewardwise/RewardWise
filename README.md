# MyTravelWallet

MyTravelWallet is an AI-powered travel rewards app that helps users decide whether to book a flight with cash, use points, or wait.

The app brings flight search, rewards value, and a conversational assistant together so users can make clearer travel booking decisions without needing to manually compare everything themselves.

## What it does

MyTravelWallet helps users:

- Search flight routes
- Compare cash prices against points redemptions
- Understand whether a redemption is worth it
- Get a simple recommendation: Pay Cash, Use Points, or Wait
- Ask Zoe, the built-in travel assistant, for help planning and comparing trips
- Review past searches and trip-related activity

## Zoe

Zoe is the AI travel assistant inside MyTravelWallet.

Users can describe a trip naturally, and Zoe helps collect the details needed to compare options, such as origin, destination, dates, cabin class, trip type, and number of travelers.

Zoe is designed to make the experience feel more conversational instead of forcing users to fill out every field manually.
